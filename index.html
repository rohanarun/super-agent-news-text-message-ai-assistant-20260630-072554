How a text message becomes real work

1. You text

Send a natural message like “reschedule my meeting and notify everyone” or “check my flight status tomorrow morning.” No prompts, no dashboards.

2. Super uses a computer

Super opens the same websites and apps you would, navigates login screens, fills forms, and follows multi-step flows — with your confirmation.

3. The computer-use cache improves it

Repeated tasks reuse a computer-use cache, so future runs are faster, cheaper, and more reliable instead of starting from scratch.

4. You stay in control

Super asks before final actions and reports back by text. No silent purchases or irreversible steps.
